Orange Cabs Terminology:

Ride = Orange Cabs Prized and Reliable Service

Riders= Orange Cabs Clientelle

Orange Cabs is a reliable metered taxi service, giving rides to passengers in and around the Gauteng area. Our service is safe and reliable, with the convenience of booking your ride via our mobile app, desktop, or by simply giving us a call.

Since its inception in 2012, Orange Cabs has grown from a 3 vehicle operation, to a full metered taxi service with multiple passenger vehicles. We offer our riders anything from single trip rides to meetings and malls to rides for groups of up to 8 passengers. All of our vehicles are less than 5 years-old and all of our drivers have valid Operators Licences. With us, you can sit back, relax and enjoy the ride, because every ride with Orange Cabs is covered by Public Liability Insurance. Our rides a quick and reliable and available to you, at the click of a button. 24/7 – 365...

Find us daily at The Boulders Mall (Midrand), Entrance 3 and Centurion Mall, Entrance 3. We are active in Johannesburg and Pretoria daily – call us, App us or hail us .
